Dynasties from 300 B.C. to 1538 A.D. in chronological order

300 B.C.

The Maurya emperor of India Ashoka the great occupied the northern part of Bengal.

1204 A.D. to 1206 A.D.

The reign and control of Bakhtiar Khiljee

1206 A.D. to 1338 A.D.

The expantion of Muslim rule throughout Bengal.

1338 A.D.

The ruler of Sonargoan Fakhruddin Mubarak Shah rebelled against the Muslim sultans and declared independence.

1538 A.D.

The independent Sultanate came to an end and Mughal Emperor Humayun occupied Gaura in Northern Bengal.
