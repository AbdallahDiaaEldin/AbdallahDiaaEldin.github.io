Chapter 1 | Planet Earth

The Earth is the third planet from the Sun and fifth largest in the Solar system. It is the only planet whose name is not drived from Greek/Latin mythology. At the start, Earth was just a ball of molten rocks. The surface cooled and condensed into water. Over millions of years, this environment helped in the development of plants. Plants carried out photsynthesis ad slowly produced nitrogen, a nonreactive gas.
